How does it work?

The parent or grandparent takes out the policy to provide for the child’s education.You decide the amount of the contribution Prudential calculates the Sum Assured i.e. the amount payable on death.

03

How long is the policy duration?

The policy duration will vary in accordance with the age of the child. You decide for how long you would like to save – the period should be between 5 and 20 years

02

Incase of untimely death or permanent disability, what happens?

At the expiry of the policy term, Prudential will pay the Maturity Value i.e. the Sum Assured plus accrued bonuses, which will be paid in three equal annual installments (with an option for lump sum payment).
